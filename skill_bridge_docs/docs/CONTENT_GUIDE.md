# SkillBridge Content & Copywriting Guide

## Brand Voice

- Empowering, clear, motivational, professional
- Friendly but not casual; expert but not intimidating

## Tone Guidelines

- Use active, positive language
- Focus on growth, progress, and opportunity
- Avoid jargon unless explained

## Page Headlines & Subtext

- **Landing:**
  - Headline: "Bridge the Gap Between Your Skills and Your Dream Job"
  - Subtext: "Identify hidden skill gaps with our AI-driven engine and follow a personalized roadmap."
- **Login/Signup:**
  - Headline: "Welcome Back"
  - Subtext: "Empower your career trajectory today."
- **Onboarding:**
  - Step 1: "What is your career goal?"
  - Step 2: "Rate your current skills"
  - Step 3: "Upload your resume"
- **Dashboard:**
  - Headline: "Welcome back, [Name]"
  - Subtext: "Your personalized career dashboard"
- **Skill Gap Analysis:**
  - Headline: "Skill Gap Analysis"
  - Subtext: "See where you stand and what to learn next."
- **Learning Path:**
  - Headline: "Your Learning Path"
  - Subtext: "A step-by-step roadmap to your target role."
- **Job Readiness Score:**
  - Headline: "Job Readiness Score"
  - Subtext: "See how close you are to your dream job."

## CTA Button Copy

- "Get Started Free"
- "Continue with Google"
- "Sign In"
- "Next Step"
- "Continue Learning"
- "Start My Free Analysis"
- "Apply Now"
- "Save Job"

## Empty State Messages

- "No skills found yet. Upload your resume to get started."
- "No job matches yet. Complete your learning path to unlock more opportunities."

## Error Messages

- "Something went wrong. Please try again."
- "Resume upload failed. Supported formats: PDF, DOCX."
- "Authentication error. Please sign in again."

## Onboarding Copy (All 3 Steps)

- Step 1: "We will personalize your skill roadmap based on this selection."
- Step 2: "Be honest — this helps us find your gaps accurately."
- Step 3: "Our AI will extract your skills automatically."

## Dashboard Welcome Messages

- "Great progress! Keep closing those gaps."
- "You’re on track for your target role."
- "Streak: [X] days — keep it going!"

## Microcopy Guidelines

- Use short, direct sentences
- Always clarify next steps
- Use tooltips for complex terms
- Confirm actions (e.g., "Resume uploaded successfully!")
